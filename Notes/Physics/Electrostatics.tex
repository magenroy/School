	2 electrons are $10^{-9}$m apart when released, what is velocity when
	they are $10^{-8}$m apart?
	\begin{gather*}
		\Delta E_k = -\Delta E_e \\
		2\frac{mv^2 + mv^2}{2} = -(E_{ef} - E_{ei}) \\
		mv^2 = kq^2\left(\frac{1}{r_i} - \frac{1}{r_f}\right) \\
		v = \sqrt{\frac{kq^2}{m}\left(\frac{1}{r_i} -
		\frac{1}{r_f}\right)}
	\end{gather*}
